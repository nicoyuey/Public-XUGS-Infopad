Summer Class - Grade 4

UNIT 1: Multiplication of Whole Numbers

  1. Identify and understand the clue words involved in multiplication
  2. Observe the correct algorithm in multiplying numbers accurately
  3. Multiply a 3-digit number by a 1 to 2 - digit number with and without regrouping
  4. Multiply a 3-digit number by a 3-digit number without / with regrouping

UNIT 2: Division of Whole Numbers

  1. Identify and understand the clue words involved in division
  2. Observe the proper way of reading and writing numbers into a division sentence
  3. Transforming horizontally written division sentences into its vertical form and vice-versa
  4. Observe the correct algorithm in dividing numbers accurately using the long method
  5. Divide a 3-digit number by a 1to 2 - digit number without / with remainder

UNIT 3: Word Problems: Multiplication & Division

  1. Understand the relationship between multiplication and division in order to solve problems with missing factors and missing dividend
  2. Analyze and transform word problems into equations.
  3. Solve word problems involving multiplication and division with correct solutions
